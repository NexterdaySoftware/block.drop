Block Drop

Objective

Clear rows for points.
Multiple rows cleared at the same time will be awarded more points.
Don't stack past the red line!

Controls

Swipe Controls (default):
Anywhere on screen swipe ←, →, ↑, or ↓ to move left, right, rotate, or drop


Button Controls:
The game area is divided into four large buttons for each of the four actions


Game Sizes:

You can play in 3 different sizes each for a unique experience:

  • Crammed-  6 wide; 4x points
  • Classic-      10 wide; 2x points
  • Spacious-   14 wide; 1x points
